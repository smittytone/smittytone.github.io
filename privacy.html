General Privacy Policy

Black Pyramid Software applications — including, but not limited to, "PreviewMarkdown", "Squinter", "FightingFantasy" and "The Valley" — do not collect, store or transmit any user data whatsoever.

Squinter requires that you enter Electric Imp Account credentials in order to access the Electric Imp impCloud™, but does not store this information itself. If, as a user, you wish to store your Electric Imp Account credentials in your computer’s system-wide keychain, Squinter enables this. However, you may choose not to store your Electric Imp Account credentials in this way.

If you are an individual located in the European Union or any jurisdiction in which the General Data Protection Regulation ("GDPR") (or a law or regulation implementing the General Data Protection Regulation 2016/679), Black Pyramid Software does not engage in any activity under GDPR.
